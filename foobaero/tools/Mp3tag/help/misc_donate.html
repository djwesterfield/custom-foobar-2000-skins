I believe that Mp3tag should be free, and I have no intention at all of commercializing Mp3tag.
 
Unfortunately, its hosting and development are not cost-free for me.
I need to buy licences for the development environment and in order to have Mp3tag running on as many platforms as possible, I also need to buy several different operating systems and test out Mp3tag on them.
 
So, I am asking you to download my software, try it, and if you like it and can spare a few bucks, please come back and make a donation. This money will be used to cover the costs of Mp3tag hosting and development, and it is absolutely voluntary!
You won't get less support or crippled versions if you don't.
 
Thank you very much!
 
 
It is also possible to send me something from my Amazon Wishlist, I would surely enjoy a surprise :) The link goes to Amazon Germany, but it should be quite easy to understand the major buttons.
